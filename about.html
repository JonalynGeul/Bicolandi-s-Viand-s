About

Every province in the Philippines may already have its own version of the dish, but we all agree that once you've tasted the authentic one from Bicol, you won't be looking at any other Bicol Express outside the region. Bicol is also known for its distinct cuisine, which mostly features the creamy coconut milk or gata, red chili peppers, and their other major crops.
In Bicol, we believe that Bicolandias Viand's are what most people are always looking for, because of its deliciousness, and spicy foods. Bicolandias Viand's specially Bicol Express, Laing, Kinunot, Sinantol, Tilmok, and Kandinga is very popular in Bicol, because of its delicious taste. Bicolandias foods is popular for their spiceness.
